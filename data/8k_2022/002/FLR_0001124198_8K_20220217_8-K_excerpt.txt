Item 1.01 Entry into a Material Definitive Agreement.

On February 17, 2022, Fluor Corporation (the “Corporation”) amended and restated its existing credit facility (the "Prior Facility") by entering into the $1,800,000,000 Third Amended and Restated Revolving Loan and Letter of Credit Facility Agreement (the "Amended Credit Facility") dated as of February 17, 2022 among the Corporation, BNP Paribas, as Administrative Agent, and other lenders party thereto.

The Amended Credit Facility extends the maturity date to February 17, 2025 and increases the size of the facility to $1.8 billion. The Amended Credit Facility contains customary financial and restrictive covenants, including a debt-to-capitalization ratio that cannot exceed 0.60 to 1.00, a limitation on the aggregate amount of debt of the greater of $750 million or €750 million for our subsidiaries, and a minimum liquidity threshold, as defined in the Amended Credit Facility, of $1.25 billion, which may be reduced to $1.00 billion upon the repayment of debt.

The foregoing description of the amendments made to the Prior Facility through the execution of the Amended Credit Facility does not purport to be complete and is qualified in its entirety by reference to the complete text of the Amended Credit Facility, a copy of which is filed as Exhibit 10.1 hereto.
