Item 8.01 Other Events.  

Notes Offering  

On February 8, 2022, News Corporation (the “Company”) announced the offering of $500,000,000 aggregate principal amount of senior notes (the “Notes”) in a private placement transaction, subject to market and other conditions (the “Notes Offering”). The Company intends to use the proceeds from the Notes Offering for general corporate purposes, including to fund the acquisitions of the Base Chemicals and Oil Price Information Service businesses from S&P Global Inc. and IHS Markit Ltd. The Company’s press release announcing the commencement of the Notes Offering is attached as Exhibit 99.1 and is incorporated herein by reference.  

The Notes being offered have not been registered under the Securities Act of 1933, as amended (the “Securities Act”), or applicable state securities laws or blue sky laws, and may not be offered or sold in the United States absent registration under the Securities Act and applicable state securities laws or available exemptions from such registration requirements. This disclosure shall not constitute an offer to sell or the solicitation of an offer to buy the Notes.
