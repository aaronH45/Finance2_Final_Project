Item 7.01. Regulation FD Disclosure.

On February 22, 2022, Cummins Inc. (“Cummins”) and Meritor, Inc. (“Meritor”) issued a joint press release announcing the entry into a definitive agreement pursuant to which Cummins will acquire Meritor for $36.50 per share in an all-cash transaction valued at $3.7 billion, including assumed debt and net of acquired cash. The full text of the press release is attached as Exhibit 99.1 and is incorporated by reference herein.

On February 22, 2022, Cummins and Meritor will hold joint investor conference calls to discuss the transaction. A copy of the presentation materials is attached hereto as Exhibit 99.2 and is incorporated by reference herein. 

In accordance with General Instruction B.2 of Form 8-K, the information in this Current Report on Form 8-K, including Exhibit 99.1 and Exhibit 99.2, shall not be deemed to be “filed” for purposes of Section 18 of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, as amended (the “Exchange Act”), or otherwise subject to the liability of that section, and shall not be incorporated by reference into any registration statement or other document filed under the Securities Act of 1933, as amended, or the Exchange Act, except as shall be expressly set forth by specific reference in such filing.
