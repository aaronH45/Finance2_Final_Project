Item 2.03. Creation of a Direct Financial Obligation or an Obligation under an Off-Balance Sheet Arrangement of a Registrant. 

On February 25, 2022, Norfolk Southern Corporation (the “Registrant”) completed its offering of $600,000,000 aggregate principal amount of its 3.000% Senior Notes due 2032 (the “2032 Notes”) and $400,000,000 aggregate principal amount of its 3.700% Senior Notes due 2053 (the “2053 Notes” and, together with the 2032 Notes, the “Notes”) pursuant to an Underwriting Agreement, dated February 15, 2022 (the “Agreement”), by and among the Registrant and Citigroup Global Markets Inc., Goldman Sachs & Co. LLC and U.S. Bancorp Investments, Inc., as representatives of the several underwriters named therein. The Notes were sold pursuant to the Registrant’s Automatic Shelf Registration Statement on Form S-3 (File No. 333-252723). The Agreement was initially filed as Exhibit 1.1 to the Registrant’s Current Report on Form 8-K filed on February 17, 2022. The description of the Agreement contained herein is qualified by reference thereto. 

The Notes were issued pursuant to an indenture, dated as of February 28, 2018, as supplemented by an eighth supplemental indenture, dated as of February 25, 2022 (the “Eighth Supplemental Indenture”), each between the Registrant and U.S. Bank Trust Company, National Association (as successor to U.S. Bank National Association), as trustee. The 2032 Notes will pay interest semi-annually in arrears at a rate of 3.000% per annum and the 2053 Notes will pay interest semi-annually in arrears at a rate of 3.700% per annum. 

The Notes may be redeemed in whole at any time or in part from time to time, at the Registrant’s option, as described below. 

If the 2032 Notes or the 2053 Notes are redeemed prior to the date that is three months prior to the maturity date for the 2032 Notes or six months prior to the maturity date for the 2053 Notes, the redemption price for such Notes to be redeemed will be equal to the greater of (1) 100% of their principal amount or (2) the sum of the present value of the remaining scheduled payments of principal and interest on the Notes to be redeemed to and including the date that is three months prior to the maturity date of the 2032 Notes or six months prior to the maturity date for the 2053 Notes (exclusive of interest accrued to, but not including, the date of redemption), discounted to the date of redemption on a semi-annual basis (assuming a 360-day year consisting of twelve 30-day months) at a specified rate, plus accrued and unpaid interest on the principal amount being redeemed to, but not including, the redemption date. 

If the 2032 Notes or the 2053 Notes are redeemed on or after the date that is three months prior to the maturity date for the 2032 Notes or six months prior to the maturity date for the 2053 Notes, the redemption price for such Notes to be redeemed will equal 100% of the principal amount of such Notes, plus accrued and unpaid interest to, but not including, the redemption date. 

The Eighth Supplemental Indenture is filed herewith as Exhibit 4.1. The description of the Eighth Supplemental Indenture contained herein is qualified by reference thereto.
