Item 2.02 Results of Operation and Financial Condition  

On January 11, 2022 Welltower Inc. (the “Company”) issued a business update presentation, which included discussion of certain developments in the Company’s senior housing operating portfolio during the quarter ended December 31, 2021, as well as a discussion of Company capital activity during this period. In addition, the presentation provides that the Company continues to anticipate normalized funds from operations (FFO) of $0.78 - $0.83 per diluted share for the quarter ended December, 31, 2021. A copy of the presentation is furnished as Exhibit 99.1 to this Current Report, and is incorporated herein by reference.
