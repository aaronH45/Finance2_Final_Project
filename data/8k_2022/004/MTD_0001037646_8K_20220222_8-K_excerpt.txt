Item 5.02. Departure of Directors or Certain Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of Certain Officers; Compensatory Arrangements of Certain Officers.

On February 22, 2022, Peter Aggersbjerg, Head of Divisions and with direct responsibility for the Laboratory Division, notified Mettler-Toledo International Inc. that he will resign in approximately one year, effective February 28, 2023, to pursue future career opportunities.

There are no immediate changes to internal reporting lines related to this announcement. Mr. Aggersbjerg will stay in his current role and work closely with the future Head of the Laboratory Division, Stefan Heiniger, (currently Head of Laboratory Weighing) on transitioning his responsibilities.
