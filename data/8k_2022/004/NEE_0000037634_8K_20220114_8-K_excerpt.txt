SECTION 8 - OTHER EVENTS

Item 8.01 Other Events

On January 14, 2022, Florida Power & Light Company sold $1.5 billion principal amount of its First Mortgage Bonds, 2.45% Series due February 3, 2032 (Bonds). The Bonds were registered under the Securities Act of 1933 pursuant to Registration Statement Nos. 333-254632, 333-254632-01 and 333-254632-02. This Current Report on Form 8-K is being filed to report as exhibits certain documents in connection with the sale of the Bonds.

In addition, on January 14, 2022, Florida Power & Light Company sold $1.0 billion principal amount of its Floating Rate Notes, Series due January 12, 2024 (Notes). The Notes will bear interest at a rate equal to Compounded SOFR (a compounded Secured Overnight Financing Rate as specified therein) plus 0.38%. The Notes were registered under the Securities Act of 1933 pursuant to Registration Statement Nos. 333-254632, 333-254632-01 and 333-254632-02. This Current Report on Form 8-K is being filed to report as exhibits certain documents in connection with the sale of the Notes.
