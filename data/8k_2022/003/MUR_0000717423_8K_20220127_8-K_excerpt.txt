Item 8.01. Other Events

On January 27, 2022, Murphy Oil Corporation (“the Company”) issued a news release, attached hereto as Exhibit 99.2, announcing that the Company’s Board of Directors declared a quarterly cash dividend on its Common Stock of $0.15 per share. The dividend is payable on March 1, 2022 to holders of record as of February 14, 2022. The dividend represents a 20 percent increase from a quarterly cash dividend of $0.125 per share paid in the fourth quarter of 2021.
