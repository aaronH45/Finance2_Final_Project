Item 5.02. Departure of Directors or Certain Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of Certain Officers; Compensatory Arrangements of Certain Officers.  

Effective February 2, 2022, the Board of Directors appointed Ron Vachris as President and Chief Operating Officer of the Company. Craig Jelinek, who had previously served as President, will continue to serve as Chief Executive Officer and a member of our Board of Directors. Concurrently with his appointment as President and Chief Operating Officer of the Company, the Board of Directors elected Mr. Vachris as a director to fill a newly-created vacancy on the Board.  

Mr. Vachris, age 56, previously served as Executive Vice President, Chief Operating Officer, Merchandising, a position he held since 2016. He previously served as Senior Vice President, Real Estate Development, from August 2015 to June 2016, and Senior Vice President, General Manager, Northwest Region, from 2010 to July 2015.
