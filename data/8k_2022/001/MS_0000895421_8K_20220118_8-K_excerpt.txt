Item 5.03. Amendments to Articles of Incorporation or Bylaws; Change in Fiscal Year. 

On January 18, 2022, Morgan Stanley (the “Registrant”) filed a Certificate of Elimination (the “Certificate of Elimination”) with the Secretary of State of the State of Delaware to eliminate its Fixed-to-Floating Rate Non-Cumulative Preferred Stock, Series H (the “Series H Preferred Stock”), which was redeemed by the Registrant on January 18, 2022. The Certificate of Elimination (a) eliminated the previous designation of 52,000 shares of Series H Preferred Stock, none of which were outstanding at the time of filing, (b) upon such elimination, caused such shares of Series H Preferred Stock to resume their status as undesignated shares of preferred stock of the Registrant, and (c) eliminated from the Amended and Restated Certificate of Incorporation of the Registrant all references to the Series H Preferred Stock. A copy of the Certificate of Elimination is filed as Exhibit 3.1 to this Current Report on Form 8-K and is incorporated herein by reference.
