 Item 8.01  

Other Events  

Willis Towers Watson Public Limited Company (the “Company”) announced that its ticker symbol on the Nasdaq stock market will change to “WTW” at the open of market trading on Monday, January 10, 2022. The Company has traded under the ticker “WLTW” since 2016. No action is required from the Company’s shareholders relative to the ticker symbol change. The Company’s ordinary shares will continue to be listed on Nasdaq and the CUSIP will remain unchanged.
