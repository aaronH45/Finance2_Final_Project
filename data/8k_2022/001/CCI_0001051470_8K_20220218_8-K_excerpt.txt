ITEM 8.01 – OTHER EVENTS  

On February 18, 2022, Crown Castle International Corp. (“Company”) notified The Bank of New York Mellon, as trustee, that it will redeem in full the $1 billion aggregate principal amount of 3.849% Senior Secured Notes due 2023 (“GS V Notes”) issued by CC Holdings GS V LLC, the Company’s indirect wholly owned subsidiary, and a subsidiary thereof as co-issuer. All of the then outstanding GS V Notes will be redeemed on March 21, 2022 at a redemption price calculated in accordance with the indenture governing the GS V Notes, plus accrued and unpaid interest on the GS V Notes. This Current Report on Form 8-K is not a notice of redemption.
