 Item 5.02(d) Election of Directors.  

 ﻿  

 This Current Report on Form 8-K/A amends certain information included in a Current Report on Form 8-K filed by Union Pacific Corporation (the Company) on November 17, 2016, regarding the election of Deborah C. Hopkins to the Board of Directors of the Company (the Board) effective January 1, 2017. The Board did not appoint Ms. Hopkins to serve on any of its committees at the time of her election. At a regular meeting of the Board held on February 2, 2017, the Board appointed Ms. Hopkins to serve on the Finance Committee and Corporate Governance and Nominating Committee, effective February 3, 2017.
