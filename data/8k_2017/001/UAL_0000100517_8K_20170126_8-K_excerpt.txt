Item 8.01 Other Events. 

On January 26, 2017, United Continental Holdings, Inc. (“UAL”) issued in a public offering $300,000,000 principal amount of its 5.00% Senior Notes due 2024 (the “Notes”), which are guaranteed (the “Guarantee”) by UAL’s wholly-owned subsidiary United Airlines, Inc. (“United”). The issuance of the Notes and the Guarantee was registered pursuant to UAL’s and United’s automatic shelf registration statement on Form S-3 (Registration Nos. 333-203630 and 333-203630-1)(the “Registration Statement”), filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission on April 24, 2015. This Current Report on Form 8-K is being filed for the purpose of adding the opinion of Hughes Hubbard & Reed LLP that the Notes and the Guarantee are binding obligations as an exhibit with respect to the Registration Statement. See Item 9.01 of this Current Report on Form 8-K.
