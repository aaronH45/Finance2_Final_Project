Item 7.01Regulation FD Disclosure

On February 22, 2017, TransDigm Group Incorporated (NYSE: TDG) announced that it has acquired the stock of SCHROTH Safety Products GmbH and certain aviation and defense assets and liabilities from subsidiaries of Takata Corporation (TYO:7312) for approximately $90 million in cash. The primary businesses purchased are that of SCHROTH Safety Products GmbH and Takata Protection Systems Inc., both of which will be known going forward as SCHROTH, which design and manufacture proprietary, highly engineered, advanced safety systems for aviation, racing and military ground vehicles throughout the world.

A copy of the February 22, 2017 press release announcing the acquisition is attached to this Current Report as Exhibit 99.1.
