 Item 5.02 Departure of Directors or Certain Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of Certain Officers; Compensatory Arrangements of Certain Officers  

On March 1, 2017, General Dynamics Corporation issued a press release announcing that Kimberly A. Kuryea, who has been vice president and controller of the company since 2011, has been appointed as senior vice president, Human Resources and Administration, and William A. Moss, who has been staff vice president, Internal Audit of the company since 2015, has been appointed as vice president and controller. The appointments are effective April 1, 2017.  

A copy of a press release issued by the company is attached as Exhibit 99.1 to this Form 8-K and incorporated herein.
