Item 8.01. Other Events. 

On January 20, 2017, Dollar Tree, Inc. (the “Company”) prepaid the $748.1 million remaining outstanding under its senior secured term loan B-3. The Company will accelerate approximately $11.9 million of amortizable non-cash deferred financing costs associated with the senior secured term loan B-3 to the fourth quarter ending January 28, 2017.

The Company may make additional prepayments on its outstanding debt from time to time in the future depending on available cash balances, free cash flow generation and projected cash needs among other factors.
