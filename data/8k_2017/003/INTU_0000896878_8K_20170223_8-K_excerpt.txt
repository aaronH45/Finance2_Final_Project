ITEM 8.01 OTHER EVENTS.

On February 23, 2017, Intuit also announced that its Board of Directors approved a cash dividend of $0.34 per share. The cash dividend will be paid on April 18, 2017 to shareholders of record as of the close of business on April 10, 2017. Future declarations of dividends and the establishment of future record dates and payment dates are subject to the final determination of the Intuit Board of Directors. A copy of the press release announcing the cash dividend is furnished as Exhibit 99.01 to this Report.
