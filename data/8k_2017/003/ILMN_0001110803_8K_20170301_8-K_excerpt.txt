Item 8.01, Other.

On March 1, 2017, GRAIL, Inc. announced that it raised over $900 million through the first close of its previously announced Series B financing. In connection with the first Series B closing, GRAIL repurchased a portion of Illumina’s stake, following which Illumina now owns slightly less than 20 percent and no longer has representation on GRAIL's board of directors. Going forward, Illumina will reflect its remaining ownership of GRAIL as a cost-method investment in Illumina's financial statements.
