5.02. Departure of Directors or Principal Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of Principal Officers.

Hewlett Packard Enterprise Company (the “Company”) has previously announced a proposed spin-off and merger of its software business segment ("HPE Software") with Micro Focus International plc, pursuant to a merger agreement dated September 7, 2016. Today, the Company announces that Chris Hsu, its Chief Operating Officer and the Executive Vice President and General Manager of HPE Software, is expected to become the Chief Executive Officer of the resulting combined entity following the completion of the transaction. Accordingly, Mr. Hsu has announced his intention to resign from his roles at the Company at such time. The spin-off and merger transaction is currently targeted to be completed by approximately August 31, 2017.
