   Item 8.01 Other Events.    

  On January 23, 2017, Conagra Brands, Inc. (the "Company") elected to exercise its optional redemption rights to redeem the entire outstanding (a) $224,795,000 aggregate principal amount of the Company’s 5.819% Senior Notes due 2017 (the "2017 Notes") and (b) $248,226,000 aggregate principal amount of the Company’s 7.000% Senior Notes due 2019 (the "2019 Notes" and, together with the 2017 Notes, the "Notes"), and The Bank of New York Mellon, as trustee under the indenture governing the Notes, issued redemption notices to registered holders of the Notes. The date fixed for the redemption of the Notes is February 22, 2017. The Notes will be redeemed at the applicable redemption prices calculated in accordance with the terms and conditions set forth in the indenture governing the Notes. The foregoing does not constitute a notice of redemption with respect to any of the Notes. The Company's previously disclosed capital allocation commitments for fiscal year 2017 remain unchanged.
