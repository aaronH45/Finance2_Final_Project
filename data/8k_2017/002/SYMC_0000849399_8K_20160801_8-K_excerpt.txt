This Amendment No. 2 on Form 8/K (“Amendment No. 2”) is being filed to amend and supplement Item 9.01 of the Current Report on Form 8-K filed by Symantec Corporation (“Symantec”) on August 1, 2016, as amended by the Current Report on Form 8-K/A filed by the Company on October 5, 2016 (“Amendment No. 1”). The Original 8-K and Amendment No. 1 were filed in connection with the completion of the Company’s acquisition of Blue Coat, Inc. (“Blue Coat”). This Amendment No. 2 is being filed to update the unaudited pro forma financial information relating to the acquisition of Blue Coat previously provided in Amendment No. 1.
