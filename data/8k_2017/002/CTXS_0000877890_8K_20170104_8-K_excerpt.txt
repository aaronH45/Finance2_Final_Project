Item 5.02 Departure of Directors or Certain Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of Certain Officers; Compensatory Arrangements of Certain Officers.  

On January 4, 2017, each of Klaus Oestermann, Senior Vice President and General Manager, Delivery Networks, and Jesse Lipson, Corporate Vice President and General Manager of Cloud Services, of Citrix Systems, Inc. (“Citrix”), announced and informed Citrix that he will be leaving the company on March 31, 2017.  

Item 8.01 Other Events.  

On January 6, 2017, Citrix issued a press release announcing details relating to the spin-off of its GoTo family of service offerings and subsequent merger of such business with a subsidiary of LogMeIn, Inc. (“LogMeIn”), a copy of which is attached as Exhibit 99.1 to this Current Report on Form 8-K. Completion of the spin-off and the merger remain subject to various closing conditions, including approval of the transaction by LogMeIn’s stockholders.
