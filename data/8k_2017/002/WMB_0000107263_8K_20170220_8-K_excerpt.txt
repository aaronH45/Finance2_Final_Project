 Item 5.02(e) Departure of Directors or Certain Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of Certain Officers; Compensatory Arrangements of Certain Officers.  

The Williams Companies, Inc. (the “Company”), has entered into an arrangement with each of its named executive officers whereby if such officer’s employment with the Company involuntarily terminates (other than for cause) on or prior to December 31, 2018, such officer shall receive two times the officer’s annual base salary plus two times their annual target bonus, and other benefits provided under the Company’s Executive Severance Pay Plan, or shall receive otherwise equivalent benefits.
