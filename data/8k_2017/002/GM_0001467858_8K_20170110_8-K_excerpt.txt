Item 7.01 Regulation FD Disclosure

On January 10, 2017 a number of General Motors Company (GM) executives presented at a conference hosted by Deutsche Bank and provided an update on GM's 2016 performance and accomplishments and 2017 outlook. In connection with the presentations GM also issued a press release. The press release and presentations related thereto are attached as Exhibits 99.1 and 99.2.

In accordance with General Instruction B.2 of Form 8-K, the information in this Item 7.01, including Exhibits 99.1 and 99.2, shall not be deemed “filed” for the purposes of Section 18 of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, as amended, or otherwise subject to the liabilities of that section. The information in this Item 7.01 shall not be incorporated by reference into any filing or other document pursuant to the Securities Act of 1933, as amended, except as shall be expressly set forth by specific reference in such filing or document.

Item 8.01 Other Events 

In addition to the existing $4.0 billion stock repurchase program to be completed through the end of 2017, on January 10, 2017 GM announced that its Board of Directors authorized the repurchase of up to an additional $5.0 billion of GM common stock to be completed at management's discretion.
