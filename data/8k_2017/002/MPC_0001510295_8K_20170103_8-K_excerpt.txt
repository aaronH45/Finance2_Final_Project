Item 7.01Regulation FD Disclosure.

On January 3, 2017, Marathon Petroleum Corporation (“MPC”) and MPLX LP will hold a webcast and conference call (the “Presentation”) to discuss updates to previously announced strategic actions. The slides attached as Exhibit 99.1 to this Current Report on Form 8-K are to be displayed during the Presentation and are incorporated herein by reference. The slides will also be available on the MPC investor relations website at http://ir.marathonpetroleum.com commencing with the live webcast of the Presentation beginning at 10 a.m. EST, on Tuesday, January 3, 2017. Information on or accessible through the MPC investor relations website is not, and shall not be deemed to be, part of this Current Report on Form 8-K.

The information in this Current Report on Form 8-K is being furnished pursuant to Item 7.01 and shall not be deemed to be “filed” for purposes of Section 18 of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, as amended, or otherwise subject to the liabilities of that section.
