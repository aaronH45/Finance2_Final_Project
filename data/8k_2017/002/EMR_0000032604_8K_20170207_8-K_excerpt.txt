Item 2.02 Results of Operations and Financial Condition

Quarterly Results Press Release

On Tuesday, February 7, 2017, a press release was issued regarding the first quarter 2017 results of Emerson Electric Co. (the “Company”). A copy of this press release is furnished with this Current Report on Form 8-K as Exhibit 99.1.

References to underlying orders in the press release refer to the Company's trailing three-month average orders growth versus the prior year, excluding currency, acquisitions and divestitures.
