 Item 8.01 Other Events.  

On January 6, 2017, Western Digital Corporation (the “Company”) issued a press release announcing the commencement by the Company of an exchange offer for all of its outstanding 10.500% Senior Notes due 2024, which are not registered under the Securities Act of 1933, as amended (the “Securities Act”), for an equal principal amount of its 10.500% Senior Notes due 2024, which have been registered under the Securities Act.
