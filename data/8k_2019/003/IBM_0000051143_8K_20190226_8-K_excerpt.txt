Item 5.02(d) Election of Directors. 

On February 26, 2019, IBM announced that Admiral Michelle Howard, retired United States Navy officer, has been elected to the IBM Board of Directors, effective March 1, 2019. Admiral Howard will join the Directors and Corporate Governance Committee.
