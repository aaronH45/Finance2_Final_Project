 Item 8.01  

Other Events.  

As previously disclosed in Electronic Arts Inc.’s (“EA’s”) Form 10-Q filing for the quarter ended September 30, 2018 filed on November 6, 2018, EA has been engaged in discussions with governmental authorities in Belgium regarding certain in-game mechanics used in the FIFA Ultimate Team service included in FIFA 18. On January 28, 2019, EA announced in a blog posting on its website at www.ea.com/news that resulting from those discussions, it has decided to stop offering FIFA Points for sale in Belgium. A copy of the blog posting is attached hereto as Exhibit 99.1. The impact of this change to FIFA Ultimate Team in Belgium is not material to our financial performance.
