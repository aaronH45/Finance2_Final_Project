Item 5.02 Departure of Directors or Certain Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of Certain Officers; Compensatory Arrangements of Certain Officers. 

In connection with Alliance Data Systems Corporation’s (“ADSC” or the “Company”) previously announced exploration of strategic alternatives for its Epsilon® business, including a potential sale of Epsilon (a “Transaction”), on January 24, 2019, the Compensation Committee of the Company’s Board of Directors approved a Transaction bonus of $1,100,000 for the Company’s Executive Vice President and President, Epsilon, Bryan J. Kennedy, to be paid within ninety days of the closing of the Transaction and contingent upon the achievement of certain quantitative and qualitative metrics with respect to the Transaction.
