 Item 8.01    

Other Events.  

On January 24, 2019, the Board of Directors (the “Board”) of Allergan plc (the “Company”) met and set a record date of March 5, 2019 for participation in the Company’s 2019 Annual General Meeting of Shareholders (the “2019 AGM”). Only those shareholders of the Company who are shareholders of record at the close of business on March 5, 2019 will be entitled to receive notice of, and to vote, at the 2019 AGM. The Board also set the date of the Company’s 2019 AGM as May 1, 2019.
