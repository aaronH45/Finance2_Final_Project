Other Events.  

Electronic Arts Inc. (“EA”) is filing this Current Report on Form 8-K to update its channels of distribution for information disclosed pursuant to Regulation FD. EA currently announces financial information and business updates through its SEC filings, press releases, public conference calls and webcasts, EA’s investor relations website (https://ir.ea.com), and EA’s news blog (https://www.ea.com/news). Going forward, EA will also use its Twitter account, @EA, to provide business updates.
