DEERE EARNINGS CALL TO BE BROADCAST ON WEBSITE 

MOLINE, IL -- (6 February 2019) Deere & Company today said its first-quarter 2019 earnings call would be broadcast over the internet on Friday, 15 February, beginning at 9:00 a.m. central time. During the call, the company’s financial and operating performance will be discussed with analysts, investors and other members of the financial community. 

The earnings call can be accessed at www.JohnDeere.com/earnings. The recorded call will be available on the Deere website for a period of time afterward. The Deere earnings release, other financial information and earnings call information slides referred to in the call, can be accessed at the link above.
