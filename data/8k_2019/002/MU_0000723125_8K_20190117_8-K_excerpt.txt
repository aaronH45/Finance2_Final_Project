Item 5.03.Amendment to Articles of Incorporation or Bylaws; Change in Fiscal Year

On January 17, 2019, the Board of Directors of the Company amended and restated the Company's bylaws (the “Amended and Restated Bylaws”) to decrease the size of the Board of Directors from nine to seven. This description of the amendment to the Company's bylaws is qualified in its entirety by reference to the text of the Amended and Restated Bylaws filed as Exhibit 99.1 to this report.
