 Item 8.01. Other Events.  

 On February 19, 2019, the Registrant announced that its Board of Directors declared a quarterly cash dividend of $0.54 per outstanding share of common stock, an increase from the previously paid dividend of $0.48 per outstanding share of common stock. The dividend will be paid on March 12, 2019 to all shareholders of record at the close of business on March 1, 2019. A copy of the Registrant’s press release is attached as Exhibit 99.2 to this Current Report on Form 8-K and is incorporated herein by reference.
