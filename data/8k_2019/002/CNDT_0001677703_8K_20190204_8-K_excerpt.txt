 Item 8.01.  

Other Events.  

On February 1, 2019, the Company, through its subsidiaries, completed the divestiture of select non-core standalone customer care contracts. The sale is a part of the Company’s previously announced plan to divest approximately $1 billion in revenue associated with non-core assets.  

The Company issued a press release on February 4, 2019, announcing the completion of the divestiture, a copy of which is filed as Exhibit 99.1 to this Current Report on Form 8-K and is incorporated herein by reference.
