Item 8.01 Other Events.

On January 10, 2019, the Board of Directors of Lennar Corporation (the “Company”) elected Fred Rothman to the position of Chief Operating Officer. Mr. Rothman will continue to oversee the Company’s East Homebuilding Region and take on an expanded role in Lennar’s strategic land acquisition program. Jon Jaffe, who previously held the title of both President and Chief Operating Officer, will continue to serve as the Company’s President and have responsibility for the Company’s operations nationally.
