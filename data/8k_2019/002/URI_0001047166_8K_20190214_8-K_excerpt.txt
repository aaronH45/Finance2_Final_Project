 Item 7.01.  

Regulation FD Disclosure.  

On February 14, 2019, United Rentals, Inc. issued a press release (attached hereto as Exhibit 99.1) announcing that members of its senior management will present at the Citi 2019 Industrials Conference on Thursday, February 21, 2019. Such presentations may include a discussion of the Company’s business, financial performance and forward-looking information.  

The presentation is scheduled to begin at 11:45 a.m. (ET) and will be broadcast live via the following link:  

http://www.veracast.com/webcasts/citigroup/industrials2019/46207334138.cfm
