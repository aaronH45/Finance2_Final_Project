Item 8.01 Other Events.  

On February 4, 2019, U.S. Bancorp issued $1,300,000,000 aggregate principal amount of its 3.375% Medium-Term Notes, Series X (Senior) due February 5, 2024 (the “Notes”). The Notes were registered under the Securities Act of 1933, as amended, pursuant to a registration statement on Form S-3 (File No. 333-217413) filed by U.S. Bancorp with the Securities and Exchange Commission. In connection with this issuance, the legal opinion as to the legality of the Notes is being filed as Exhibit 5.1 to this Current Report on Form 8-K.
