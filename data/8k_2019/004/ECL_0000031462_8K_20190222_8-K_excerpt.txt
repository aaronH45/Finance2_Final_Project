 (b) Resignation of a Director  

 On February 22, 2019, the board of directors of Ecolab Inc. (the “Company”) accepted the resignation of Stephen I. Chazen from the board of directors, including his roles on the Company’s audit and finance committees, to be effective as of the Company’s Annual Meeting of Stockholders on May 2, 2019. Mr. Chazen tendered his resignation upon attaining the age of 72 in accordance with the Company’s Corporate Governance Principles, and his resignation was not due to any disagreement with the Company or its management with respect to any matter relating to the Company’s operations, policies or practices.
