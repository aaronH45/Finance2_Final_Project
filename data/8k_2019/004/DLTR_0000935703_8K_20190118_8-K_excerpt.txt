Item 8.01. Other Events. 

On January 18, 2019, Dollar Tree, Inc. (the “Company”) prepaid the $782.0 million outstanding under its Term Loan Facility. The Company accelerated approximately $1.5 million of amortizable non-cash deferred financing costs associated with the Term Loan Facility to the fourth quarter ended February 2, 2019.

The Company may make additional prepayments on its outstanding debt from time to time in the future depending on available cash balances, free cash flow generation and projected cash needs among other factors.
