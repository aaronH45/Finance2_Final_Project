 Item 5.02.  Departure of Directors or Certain Officers; Election of Directors; 

 Appointment of Certain Officers; Compensatory Arrangements of Certain Officers. 

On February 5, 2019, Foot Locker, Inc. issued a press release announcing that, as part of a global realignment, Lewis P. Kimble, Executive Vice President and Chief Executive Officer—International, a named executive officer, has been appointed to the new role of Executive Vice President and Chief Executive Officer—Asia Pacific. The appointment is effective March 11, 2019. 

Also, as part of the global realignment, Vijay Talwar has been promoted to Executive Vice President and Chief Executive Officer—EMEA. 

A copy of the press release is furnished as Exhibit 99.1, which, in its entirety, is incorporated herein by reference.
