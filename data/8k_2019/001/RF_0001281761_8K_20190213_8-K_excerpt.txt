Item 7.01Regulation FD Disclosure.

On February 13, 2019, Regions Financial Corporation ("Regions" or the "Company") issued a press release announcing that it will host an Investor Day Conference for investors and analysts on Wednesday, February 27, 2019 in New York City. Regions' senior executives will review and discuss the Company's business strategies beginning at 8 a.m. and ending at approximately 12:30 p.m. EST.

A live video webcast and the presentation materials referenced during the conference will be available on the Investor Relations page of Regions' website at www.regions.com. Following the event, archived copies of the video webcast and presentation materials will be available on the same site for at least the next 12 months. The Company intends to file another Form 8-K with an exhibit containing all of the presented materials from the conference at or around the conclusion of the event.
