 Item 5.03.Amendments to Articles of Incorporation or Bylaws; Change in Fiscal Year. 

On February 7, 2019, the Board of Directors of Delta Air Lines, Inc. (“Delta”) approved an amendment to Delta’s Bylaws to reduce the percentage of voting power required to call a special meeting of stockholders from 40% to 20%. 

The description of the amendment to the Bylaws above is qualified in its entirety to the text of the Bylaws, as amended and restated through February 7, 2019, which are attached as Exhibit 3.1 to this Form 8-K and incorporated herein by reference. The amendment to the Bylaws was effective on February 7, 2019.
