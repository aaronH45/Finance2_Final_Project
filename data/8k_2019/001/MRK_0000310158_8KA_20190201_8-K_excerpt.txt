EXPLANATORY NOTE 

Merck & Co., Inc. (Merck) is filing this Amendment No. 1 to its Current Report on Form 8-K filed with the SEC on February 1, 2019 (the Original Form 8-K), to refurnish Exhibit 99.2 in order to correct a typographical error in Table 3b thereto in which the Global Full Year 2018 sales figure for Isentress / Isentress HD was incorrectly stated to be $1,410 million rather than $1,140 million. This Amendment No. 1 is filed solely to refurnish Exhibit 99.2 to correct such typographical error. No changes to the other documents furnished with the Original Form 8-K have been made.
