(b) Retirement of Directors  

On February 26, 2019, James McCann announced his intention to retire from the Board of Directors of Willis Towers Watson Public Limited Company (the “Company”). He will serve the remainder of his term and will not seek re-election at the 2019 Annual General Meeting of Shareholders (the “2019 AGM”). Accordingly, at the 2019 AGM (currently anticipated to be on May 20, 2019), he will retire from the Board and all Board Committees. Mr. McCann’s retirement follows almost 15 years of service on the Board and various Board Committees, with almost seven years of service as Non-Executive Chairman or Lead Independent Director of the Company and its Willis predecessor. This includes serving as Non-Executive Chairman during the three-year period following the merger of Willis and Towers Watson.
