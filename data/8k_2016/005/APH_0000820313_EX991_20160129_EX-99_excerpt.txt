AMPHENOL ANNOUNCES 

FIRST QUARTER 2016 DIVIDEND PAYMENT 

Wallingford, Connecticut, January 29, 2016. Amphenol Corporation (NYSE:APH) confirmed today that its Board of Directors approved the first quarter 2016 dividend on its Common Stock in the amount of $.14 per share at its meeting held on January 28, 2016. The Company will pay this first quarter 2016 dividend on or about April 6, 2016 to shareholders of record as of March 15, 2016.

Amphenol has a diversified presence as a leader in high growth areas of the interconnect market including: Automotive, Broadband Communications, Commercial Aerospace, Industrial, Information Technology and Data Communications, Military, Mobile Devices and Mobile Networks.
