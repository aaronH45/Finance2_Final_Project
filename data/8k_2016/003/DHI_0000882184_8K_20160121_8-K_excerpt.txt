Item 5.07. Submission of Matters to a Vote of Security Holders.

On January 21, 2016, D.R. Horton, Inc. (the “Company”) held its Annual Meeting of Stockholders (the “Annual Meeting”). At the Annual Meeting, stockholders considered: (1) the election of five director nominees named in the Company’s proxy statement, (2) an advisory vote on the approval of executive compensation, and (3) the ratification of the appointment of PricewaterhouseCoopers LLP as the Company’s independent registered public accounting firm for fiscal 2016. There were 369,570,908 shares of Common Stock eligible to be voted at this meeting and there were 336,408,127 shares of Common Stock represented in person or by proxy.
