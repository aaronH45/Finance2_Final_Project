Item 2.02 Results of Operations and Financial Condition

On January 14, 2016, JPMorgan Chase & Co. (“JPMorgan Chase” or the “Firm”) reported 2015 fourth quarter net income of $5.4 billion, or $1.32 per share, compared with net income of $4.9 billion, or $1.19 per share, in the fourth quarter of 2014.
