Item 5.02(b). Departure of Named Executive Officer. 

On December 28, 2015, Steven A. Mills, Executive Vice President, Software & Systems, age 64, decided to retire from the Company on December 31, 2015, after a 42-year career with IBM. 

Item 5.03(a). Amendment to Bylaws. 

On September 28, 2015, IBM announced that Hutham S. Olayan was elected to the IBM Board of Directors, effective January 1, 2016. Article III, Section 2 of IBM’s bylaws was amended to increase the number of directors to fifteen, effective January 1, 2016. Exhibit 3.2 of this Form 8-K contains IBM’s amended bylaws. 

IBM’s web site (www.ibm.com) contains a significant amount of information about IBM, including financial and other information for investors (www.ibm.com/investor/). IBM encourages investors to visit its various web sites from time to time, as information is updated and new information is posted.
