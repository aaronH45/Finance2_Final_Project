 Invesco Ltd. Announces December 31, 2015 

 Assets Under Management

 Invesco Relations Contact: Jordan Krugman 404-439-4605

 Media Relations Contact: Graham Galt 404-439-3070

Atlanta, January 12, 2016 --- Invesco Ltd. (NYSE: IVZ) today reported preliminary month-end assets under management (AUM) of $775.6 billion, a decrease of 2.0% month over month. The decrease was driven by unfavorable market returns, lower money market AUM, negative foreign exchange, and net long-term outflows. FX decreased AUM by $2.5 billion. Preliminary average total AUM for the quarter through December 31 were $783.7 billion, and preliminary average active AUM for the quarter through December 31 were $644.6 billion.
