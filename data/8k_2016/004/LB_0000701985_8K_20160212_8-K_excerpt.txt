Item 5.02 Departure of Directors or Principal Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of Principal Officers. 

On February 12, 2016 the Company announced the resignation of Sharen Jester Turney as President and CEO of Victoria’s Secret. Leslie H. Wexner, Chairman and CEO of L Brands, has assumed leadership of Victoria’s Secret. Ms. Turney will be entitled to certain payments and benefits under Section 10(b) of her employment agreement, as previously disclosed in our annual proxy statements, and will continue in an advisory role. 

A copy of the press release announcing Ms. Turney’s resignation is attached hereto as Exhibit 99.1 and is incorporated herein by reference.
