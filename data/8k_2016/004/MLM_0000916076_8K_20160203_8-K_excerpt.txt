Item 1.04 Mine Safety—Reporting of Shutdowns and Patterns of Violations.  

On February 3, 2016, the Corporation received an order issued by the Mine Safety and Health Administration (“MSHA”) under section 107(a) of the Federal Mine Safety and Health Act of 1977 at its Weeping Water Mine in Weeping Water, Nebraska. The order stated that an operator of a haul truck lost traction of the vehicle while driving up a ramp and slid into a berm. No one was injured in the cited incident. The Corporation took corrective action.
