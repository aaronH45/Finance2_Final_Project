 Item 7.01 Regulation FD Disclosure.  

The J. M. Smucker Company (the “Company”) today announced that it completed the divestiture of its U.S. canned milk brands and operations to Eagle Family Foods Group LLC, a subsidiary of funds affiliated with Kelso & Company, on December 31, 2015. The Company previously announced the transaction with Eagle Family Foods Group LLC on November 3, 2015. The transaction included canned milk products that are primarily sold in U.S. retail and foodservice channels under the Eagle Brand® and Magnolia® brands, along with other branded and private label trade names, with annual net sales of approximately $200 million. The Company’s manufacturing facilities in El Paso, Texas and Seneca, Missouri were included in the transaction. The transaction did not include the Company’s canned milk business in Canada.
