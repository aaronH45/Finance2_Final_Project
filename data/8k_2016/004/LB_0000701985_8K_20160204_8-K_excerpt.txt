Item 8.01. Other Events.

On February 4, 2016, L Brands, Inc. announced that its Board of Directors authorized a new $500 million share repurchase program, which includes the remaining $17 million outstanding under the previous program. L Brands, Inc. also increased its annual ordinary dividend to $2.40 per share and declared a $2.00 per share special dividend, both of which will be paid on March 4, 2016, to shareholders of record at the close of business on February 19, 2016.
