COSTCO WHOLESALE CORPORATION ANNOUNCES  

QUARTERLY CASH DIVIDEND  

ISSAQUAH, Wash., January 29, 2016 - Costco Wholesale Corporation (“Costco” or the “Company”) (Nasdaq: COST) today announced that its Board of Directors declared a quarterly cash dividend on Costco common stock of $.40 per share. The quarterly dividend is payable February 26, 2016, to shareholders of record at the close of business on February 12, 2016.  

Costco currently operates 698 warehouses, including 488 in the United States and Puerto Rico, 90 in Canada, 36 in Mexico, 27 in the United Kingdom, 24 in Japan, 12 in Korea, 11 in Taiwan, eight in Australia and two in Spain. Costco also operates electronic commerce web sites in the U.S., Canada, the United Kingdom, Mexico, Korea and Taiwan.
