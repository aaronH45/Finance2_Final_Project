 Item 5.02 Departure of Directors or Certain Officers; Appointment of Certain Officers; Compensatory Arrangements of Certain Officers.  

On February 24, 2016, Citizens Financial Group, Inc. (the “Company”) announced the appointment of Malcolm Griggs as Executive Vice President and Chief Risk Officer effective April 1, 2016. Mr. Griggs, who has been with the Company since 2014, will succeed the Company’s current Chief Risk Officer, Nancy Shanik, who is retiring from the Company as of May 31, 2016.  

A copy of the Company’s press release announcing Mr. Grigg’s appointment and Ms. Shanik’s retirement is attached to this report as Exhibit 99.1 and is incorporated herein by reference.
