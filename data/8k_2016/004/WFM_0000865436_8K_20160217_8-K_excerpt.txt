Item 8.01 Other Events 

On February 17, 2016, Mr. Hass Hassan, director of the Company, adopted a stock trading plan in accordance with the guidelines specified in Rule 10b5-1 under the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, as amended, and the Company’s insider trading policy. Mr. Hassan’s trading plan is part of his asset diversification, tax and financial planning strategy. Under the trading plan, if the stock price is above specified levels, a brokerage firm will be authorized to sell up to 3,119 shares of common stock of the Company. Sales under the plan are scheduled to take place between February 17, 2016 and August 12, 2016. Transactions under the trading plan will be disclosed publicly through Form 4 filings with the Securities and Exchange Commission to the extent required by law.
