 Item 5.02  Departure of Directors or Certain Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of Certain Officers; Compensatory Arrangements of Certain Officers   

On December 31, 2015, Ronald E. Deal and John P. Howe III, M.D. retired from the board of directors of BB&T Corporation (“BB&T”) pursuant to BB&T’s director retirement policy. 

Effective January 1, 2016, the board designated Jennifer S. Banner as BB&T's lead director, succeeding Mr. Deal in that role upon his retirement.
