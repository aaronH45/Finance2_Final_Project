Item 5.02Departure of Directors or Certain Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of Certain Officers; Compensatory Arrangements of Certain Officers.

On January 12, 2015, Francis S. Blake, Chairman of The Home Depot, Inc. (the “Company”), informed the Board of Directors of the Company (the “Board”) that he will retire from the Company and the Board effective as of the end of the Company’s 2014 fiscal year on February 1, 2015. On January 15, 2015, the Board elected Chief Executive Officer and President Craig A. Menear as Chairman, effective February 2, 2015. 

A copy of the press release announcing these changes is attached hereto as Exhibit 99.1.
