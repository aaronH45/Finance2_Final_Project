ITEM 7.01 – REGULATION FD DISCLOSURE  

On February 16, 2015, Crown Castle International Corp. (“Company”) issued a press release announcing that the Company plans to explore the potential sale of its Australian subsidiary. The February 16, 2015 press release is furnished herewith as Exhibit 99.1 to this Form 8-K.
