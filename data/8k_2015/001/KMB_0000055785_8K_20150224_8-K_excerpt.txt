Item 8.01 Other Events. 

On February 24, 2015, Kimberly-Clark Corporation (the “Corporation”) entered into an Underwriting Agreement (the “Underwriting Agreement”) with Citigroup Global Markets Inc., Deutsche Bank Securities Inc., J.P. Morgan Securities LLC and Morgan Stanley & Co. LLC, as representatives for the several underwriters named therein, for the issuance and sale by the Corporation of $250,000,000 aggregate principal amount of 1.850% Notes due March 1, 2020 (the “2020 Notes”) and $250,000,000 aggregate principal amount of 2.650% Notes due March 1, 2025 (together with the 2020 Notes, the “Notes”). The Notes were registered under the Securities Act of 1933, as amended, pursuant to the Corporation’s registration statement on Form S-3 (File No. 333-189633) filed on June 27, 2013. 

A copy of the Underwriting Agreement is attached hereto as Exhibit 1.1. Forms of the Notes are attached hereto as Exhibits 4.1 and 4.2.
