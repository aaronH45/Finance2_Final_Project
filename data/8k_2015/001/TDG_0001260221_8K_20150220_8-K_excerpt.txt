Item 1.01 Entry into a Material Definitive Agreement  

As previously reported, on February 20, 2015, TransDigm Group Incorporated entered into a definitive agreement (the “Agreement”) to purchase the Telair Cargo Group of businesses (“Telair”), a global leader in aerospace on-board cargo loading and handling, restraint systems and unit load devices from AAR CORP, for a total purchase price of approximately $725 million in cash, subject to adjustment.  

The Agreement is attached to this Report as Exhibit 2.1.
