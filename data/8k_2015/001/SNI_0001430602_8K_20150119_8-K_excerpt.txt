 Item 5.02. Departure of Directors or Certain Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of Certain Officers; Compensatory Arrangements of Certain Officers.  

On January 19, 2015, Nackey E. Scagliotti, a director of Scripps Networks Interactive Inc. (the “Company”) since 2008, notified the Company’s board of directors that she intends to retire and will not stand for reelection at the Company’s 2015 Annual Meeting of Shareholders set for May 12, 2015. Mrs. Scagliotti is the former Chairman of the Board of The E.W. Scripps Company and former Chairman of the Board of Directors and Assistant Publisher of The Union Leader Corporation. The Board would like to thank Mrs. Scagliotti for her many years of dedicated and loyal service to the Company.
