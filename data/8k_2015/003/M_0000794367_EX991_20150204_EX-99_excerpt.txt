text-autospace:none'><b>MACY'S, INC.
EXPECTS FULL-YEAR 2014 EARNINGS PER SHARE </b></p>

<p style='margin:0in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman","serif";text-align:center;page-break-after:avoid;
text-autospace:none'><b>TO EXCEED
PREVIOUS GUIDANCE </b></p>

<p style='margin:0in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman","serif";text-align:center;page-break-after:avoid;
text-autospace:none'><b><i>Fourth quarter
comparable sales growth in line with expectations </i></b></p>

<p style='margin:0in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman","serif";text-align:center;page-break-after:avoid;
text-autospace:none'><b>&nbsp;</b></p>

<p style='margin-bottom:.0001pt;font-family:"Times New Roman","serif";text-indent:.5in;text-autospace:none; margin-left:0in; margin-right:0in; margin-top:0in'>CINCINNATI, Ohio, February 3, 2015 &#150; Macy's, Inc.
today reported preliminary earnings per share for the full-year 2014 that are
above previous guidance, as well as fourth quarter 2014 comparable sales
consistent with expectations.&nbsp; </p>

<p style='margin-bottom:.0001pt;font-family:"Times New Roman","serif";text-indent:.5in;text-autospace:none; margin-left:0in; margin-right:0in; margin-top:0in'>&nbsp;</p>

<p style='mso-style-link:"Body Text 2 Char";margin-bottom:.0001pt;line-height:12.0pt;text-autospace:none;font-family:"Times New Roman","serif";color:black; margin-left:0in; margin-right:0in; margin-top:0in'><i>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; (Editor's Note: Macy's, Inc. this
afternoon also issued separate news releases announcing senior executive
appointments to develop and drive growth strategies and an agreement to acquire Bluemercury, Inc.)</i></p>

<p style='margin-bottom:.0001pt;font-family:"Times New Roman","serif";text-align:center;page-break-after:avoid;
text-autospace:none; margin-left:0in; margin-right:0in; margin-top:0in'><b>&nbsp;</b></p>

<p style='margin-bottom:.0001pt;font-family:"Times New Roman","serif";margin-bottom:15.0pt;text-indent:.5in; margin-left:0in; margin-right:0in; margin-top:0in'>&#147;We are very pleased with our fourth
quarter performance, which represents a strengthening trend from the third
quarter and spring season,&#148; said Terry J. Lundgren, chairman and chief
executive officer of Macy's, Inc. &#147;In particular, we believe that we have an
excellent foundation on which to continue to grow sales and earnings, as
described in last month's moves to restructure our merchandising and marketing
organizations, and today's announcements on new senior executive appointments
and the agreement to acquire Bluemercury, Inc.&#148; </p>

<p style='margin-bottom:.0001pt;font-family:"Times New Roman","serif";margin-bottom:15.0pt;text-indent:.5in; margin-left:0in; margin-right:0in; margin-top:0in'>The company currently expects full-year
2014 earnings of between $4.35 and $4.37 per diluted share, which is higher
than guidance (revised on Jan. 8, 2015) in the range of $4.25 to $4.35 per
diluted share. This estimate excludes charges of approximately $100 million to
$110 million related to the previously announced merchandising and marketing
restructuring, store and field adjustments, store closings and asset impairment
charges, as well as approximately $17 million of interest expense related to
the make-whole premium for the previously announced early retirement of debt. </p>

<p style='margin-bottom:.0001pt;font-family:"Times New Roman","serif";margin-bottom:15.0pt;text-indent:.5in; margin-left:0in; margin-right:0in; margin-top:0in'>Comparable sales growth on an owned plus
licensed basis in the fourth quarter of 2014 was 2.5 percent (in line with
guidance provided on Jan. 8, 2015, of 2.5 percent to 3 percent). This calculates
to growth in comparable sales on an owned plus licensed basis in the full-year
2014 of 1.4 percent. On an owned basis, fourth quarter comparable sales were
2.0 percent, (in line with previous guidance of 1.9 percent to 2.4 percent).
This calculates to growth of comparable sales on an annual basis of 0.7 percent
on an owned basis. </p>

<p style='margin-bottom:.0001pt;font-family:"Times New Roman","serif";margin-bottom:15.0pt;text-indent:.5in; margin-left:0in; margin-right:0in; margin-top:0in'>Please see the last page of this news
release for additional information.</p>

<p style='margin-bottom:.0001pt;font-family:"Times New Roman","serif";margin-bottom:15.0pt;text-indent:.5in; margin-left:0in; margin-right:0in; margin-top:0in'>Macy's, Inc. is scheduled to report fourth quarter
sales and earnings on Tuesday, Feb. 24, 2015. Additional detail on financial performance will be provided at that time. The company will webcast a call with financial analysts and investors at 10:30 a.m. ET on
Feb. 24. Macys, Inc.'s webcast is accessible to the media and general public via the company's website at www.macysinc.com. Analysts and investors may call in on 1-800-835-9927, passcode 8920673. A replay of the
conference call can be accessed on the website or by calling 1-888-203-1112 (same passcode) about two hours after the conclusion of the call.

<p style='margin-bottom:.0001pt;font-family:"Times New Roman","serif";text-indent:.5in;text-autospace:none; margin-top:0in'>&nbsp;</p>

<p style='margin-bottom:.0001pt;font-family:"Times New Roman","serif";text-indent:.5in;text-autospace:none; margin-left:0in; margin-right:0in; margin-top:0in'>Macy's, Inc., with corporate offices in Cincinnati and
New York, is one of the nation's premier retailers, with fiscal 2013 sales of $27.931
billion. The company operates about 825 stores in 45 states, the District of
Columbia, Guam and Puerto Rico under the names of Macy's and Bloomingdale's, as
well as the macys.com and bloomingdales.com websites. The company also operates
13 Bloomingdale's Outlet stores. Bloomingdale&#146;s in Dubai is operated by Al Tayer Group LLC under a license agreement.</p><p style='margin-bottom:.0001pt;font-family:"Times New Roman","serif";text-indent:.5in;text-autospace:none; margin-left:0in; margin-right:0in; margin-top:0in'>&nbsp;</p>
