 Item 8.01. Other Events  

On February 27, 2015, JPMorgan Chase & Co. (the “Company”) issued a press release announcing the redemption on April 2, 2015 of the issued and outstanding $1,500,000,000 aggregate liquidation amount of the 6.70% Capital Securities, Series CC (the “Trust Preferred Securities”) issued by JPMorgan Chase Capital XXIX pursuant to the optional redemption provisions provided in the documents governing such Trust Preferred Securities. Under applicable regulatory capital requirements issued by the Federal Reserve, upon notice of redemption, the Trust Preferred Securities will no longer qualify as Tier 1 capital for the Company. The redemptions will be funded with available cash.  

A copy of the press release referenced above is attached hereto as Exhibit 99.1.
