 Assets Under Management

Atlanta, January 12, 2015 --- Invesco Ltd. (NYSE: IVZ) today reported preliminary month-end assets under management (AUM) of $792.4 billion, a decrease of 1.4% month over month. The decrease was driven by unfavorable market returns, net outflows in PowerShares QQQ’s, and negative foreign exchange. FX decreased AUM by $1.6 billion during the month and long-term net flows were flat. Preliminary average total AUM for the quarter through December 31 were $789.8 billion, and preliminary average active AUM for the quarter through December 31 were $647.6 billion.
