Item 5.02. Departure of Directors or Certain Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of Certain Officers; Compensatory Arrangements of Certain Officers. 

On February 19, 2015, current Directors James D. Robinson III and Peter V. Ueberroth each provided notice to the Chairman of the Board of Directors of The Coca-Cola Company (the “Company”) of his intention not to stand for re-election at the 2015 Annual Meeting of Shareowners. Messrs. Robinson and Ueberroth will both remain on the Board and maintain their respective Committee memberships until the 2015 Annual Meeting of Shareowners, which is scheduled to occur in April 2015. The Company’s February 19, 2015 press release announcing the retirement of Messrs. Robinson and Ueberroth is attached hereto as Exhibit 99.1 and incorporated herein by reference.
