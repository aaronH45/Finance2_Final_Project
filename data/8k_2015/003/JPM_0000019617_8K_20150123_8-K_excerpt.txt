Item 8.01. Other Events  

On January 23, 2015, JPMorgan Chase & Co. closed a public offering of $2,500,000,000 aggregate principal amount of its 2.250% Notes due 2020 and $2,500,000,000 aggregate principal amount of its 3.125% Notes due 2025 (together, the “Fixed Rate Notes”) and a public offering of $1,150,000,000 aggregate principal amount of its Floating Rate Notes due 2020 (the “Floating Rate Notes” and, together with the Fixed Rate Notes, the “Notes”). The Notes were registered under the Securities Act of 1933, as amended, pursuant to a registration statement on Form S-3 (File No. 333-191692). In connection with these offerings, the legal opinion as to the legality of the Fixed Rate Notes is being filed as Exhibit 5.1 to this report and the legal opinion as to the legality of the Floating Rate Notes is being filed as Exhibit 5.2 to this report.
