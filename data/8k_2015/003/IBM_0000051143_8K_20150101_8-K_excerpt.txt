Item 5.02 Departure of Certain Officers and Election of Certain Officers 

On January 6, 2015, IBM announced that James J. Kavanaugh, Vice President and Controller, became Senior Vice President, Transformation and Operations, effective January 1, 2015. In addition, Stanley J. Sutula III became Vice President and Controller, effective January 1, 2015. Mr. Sutula, 49, was most recently the Vice President and Treasurer at IBM. He has been an IBM executive for over 13 years. Simon Beaumont became Vice President and Treasurer, effective January 1, 2015. 

Item 5.03(a) Amendment to Bylaws 

On July 29, 2014, IBM announced that Peter R. Voser was elected to the IBM Board of Directors, effective January 1, 2015. Article III, Section 2 of IBM’s bylaws was amended to increase the number of directors to fourteen, effective January 1, 2015. Exhibit 3.2 of this Form 8-K contains IBM’s amended bylaws.
