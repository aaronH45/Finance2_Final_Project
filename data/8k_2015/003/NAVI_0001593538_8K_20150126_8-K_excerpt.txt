 ITEM 8.01 Other Events.  

On January 26, 2015, the Board of Directors of Navient Corporation (the “Company”) approved a seven percent (7%) increase in the Company’s 2015 first quarter dividend, increasing the dividend to $0.16 per share of the Company’s common stock. The first quarter dividend will be paid on March 20, 2015, to shareholders of record at the close of business on March 6, 2015.  

A copy of the Company’s press release announcing the dividend increase is attached hereto as Exhibit 99.1 and incorporated herein by reference.
