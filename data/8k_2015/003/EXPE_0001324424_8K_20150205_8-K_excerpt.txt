On February 5, 2015, Expedia announced that its Executive Committee, acting on behalf of its Board of Directors, has (i) declared a quarterly cash dividend of $0.18 per share of outstanding common stock payable on March 26, 2015 to stockholders of record as of the close of business on March 10, 2015, and (ii) authorized an additional repurchase of up to 10 million shares of the Company’s common stock.
