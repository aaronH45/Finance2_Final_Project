 Item 8.01 Other Events.  

On January 27, 2015, Registrant announced that its Board of Directors has approved a new share repurchase plan that authorizes the repurchase of up to 20 million shares of Registrant’s stock, which replaces its existing share repurchase plan. The shares may be repurchased from time to time in the open market or in privately negotiated transactions at prices TSYS deems appropriate, subject to market conditions, business opportunities and other factors. This authorization has no expiration date and may be suspended or terminated at any time.
