Item 8.01 Other Events.  

On February 24, 2015, in connection with the Registration Statement on Form S-3 (File No. 333-181185), effective May 4, 2012, Health Care REIT, Inc. (the “Company”) entered into an Underwriting Agreement with Goldman, Sachs & Co., Barclays Capital Inc., UBS Securities LLC and Wells Fargo Securities, LLC, as representatives of the several underwriters (the “Underwriters”), for an offering of 17,000,000 shares of common stock of the Company. The Underwriters exercised their option to purchase additional shares in full, so the total number of shares to be issued in the offering is 19,550,000. The offering is expected to close on February 27, 2015, subject to the satisfaction of customary closing conditions.
