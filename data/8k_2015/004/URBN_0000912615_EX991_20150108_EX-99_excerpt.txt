Urban Outfitters Reports Record Holiday Sales  

PHILADELPHIA, PA, January 8, 2015 - Urban Outfitters, Inc. (NASDAQ:URBN), a leading lifestyle specialty retail company operating under the Anthropologie, Bhldn, Free People, Terrain and Urban Outfitters brands, today announced record net sales for the two months ended December 31, 2014.  

Total Company net sales for the two months increased to $785 million or 10% over the same period last year. Comparable Retail segment net sales, which include our comparable direct-to-consumer channel, increased 4%. Comparable Retail segment net sales increased 17% at Free People, 5% at the Anthropologie Group and 1% at Urban Outfitters. Wholesale segment net sales increased 14%.  

“This year’s holiday environment in the fashion apparel industry was more promotional than any I can recall,” said Richard A. Hayne, Chief Executive Officer. “Thus, I am pleased the company delivered record sales and positive retail segment comps at each of our brands,” finished Mr. Hayne.  

For the 11-month period ended December 31, 2014, total Company net sales increased to $3.1 billion or 7% over the same period last year. Comparable Retail segment net sales increased 1%. Wholesale segment net sales increased 27%.  

During the 11 months ended December 31, 2014, the Company opened a total of 37 new stores including: 14 Anthropologie stores, 12 Free People stores, and 11 Urban Outfitters stores; and closed 1 Urban Outfitters store.  

Urban Outfitters, Inc. is an innovative specialty retail company which offers a variety of lifestyle merchandise to highly defined customer niches through 240 Urban Outfitters stores in the United States, Canada, and Europe, catalogs and websites; 203 Anthropologie Group stores in the United States, Canada and Europe, catalogs and websites; 102 Free People stores in the United States and Canada, catalogs and websites; Free People wholesale, which sells its product to approximately 1,500 specialty stores and select department stores worldwide; and 2 Terrain garden centers and a website, as of December 31, 2014.
