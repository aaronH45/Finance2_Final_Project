Item 8.01 Other Events 

On January 15, 2015, United Technologies Corporation (the "Company") announced that it will streamline its aerospace organization and leadership structure to provide greater focus on serving customers. It also announced that Alain M. Bellemare, President & Chief Executive Officer of UTC Propulsion & Aerospace Systems, has elected to leave the Company at the end of January 2015. He will serve as a consultant to the Company. 

A copy of the related press release is attached as Exhibit 99.1 to this Form 8-K and is incorporated herein by reference.
