AMPHENOL ANNOUNCES 

FIRST QUARTER 2015 DIVIDEND PAYMENT 

Wallingford, Connecticut, January 30, 2015. Amphenol Corporation (NYSE:APH) confirmed today that its Board of Directors approved the first quarter 2015 dividend on its Common Stock in the amount of $.125 per share at its meeting held on January 29, 2015. The Company will pay this first quarter 2015 dividend on or about April 1, 2015 to shareholders of record as of March 11, 2015.
