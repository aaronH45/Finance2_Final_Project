 Item 5.02. Departure of Directors or Certain Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of Certain Officers; Compensatory Arrangements of Certain Officers.  

(d) On February 24, 2015, the board of directors (the “Board”) of Wells Fargo & Company (the “Company”) elected Suzanne M. Vautrinot to the Company’s Board, effective immediately. Ms. Vautrinot is considered an independent director under the New York Stock Exchange’s corporate governance listing standards and the Company’s Corporate Governance Guidelines. Ms. Vautrinot has been appointed to serve on the Board’s Audit and Examination Committee.  

Ms. Vautrinot will receive customary fees and equity awards from the Company for serving as a director in accordance with the Company’s non-employee director compensation program as described in the Company’s 2014 proxy statement filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission on March 18, 2014. Under the non-employee director compensation program, on February 24, 2015, Ms. Vautrinot was automatically granted a stock award under the Company’s Long-Term Incentive Compensation Plan of 723 shares of Company common stock with a fair market value of approximately $40,000 on the date of grant, which she elected to defer as common stock units under the Company’s director deferral program. The Company common stock units were fully vested on the date of grant. Additional information regarding equity awards under the non-employee director compensation program and our director deferral program under the Wells Fargo & Company Directors Stock Compensation and Deferral Plan is included in Exhibits 10(q), 10(r) and 10(f) of the Company’s Annual Report on Form 10-K for the year ended December 31, 2014.  

The Company’s news release (the “News Release”) announcing the election of Ms. Vautrinot to the Board is attached hereto as Exhibit 99.1 and is incorporated by reference into this Item 5.02.
