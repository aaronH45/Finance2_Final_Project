Item 5.02. Departure of Directors or Certain Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of Certain Officers; Compensatory Arrangements of Certain Officers

On January 7, 2015, Raytheon Company (the “Company”) announced that David C. Wajsgras, Senior Vice President and Chief Financial Officer of the Company, has been appointed to serve as President, Raytheon Intelligence, Information and Services, effective March 2, 2015, succeeding Lynn A. Dugle, who has announced plans to retire from the Company. The Company also announced that Anthony F. O’Brien has been appointed to serve as Vice President and Chief Financial Officer effective March 2, 2015, succeeding Mr. Wajsgras. 

Mr. O’Brien joined the Company in 1986, and has held numerous finance positions of increasing responsibility with the Company. He currently serves as Vice President and Chief Financial Officer for the Raytheon Integrated Defense Systems business.
