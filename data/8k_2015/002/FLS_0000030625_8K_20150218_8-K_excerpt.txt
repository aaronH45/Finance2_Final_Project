Item 5.02 Departure of Directors or Certain Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of Certain Officers; Compensatory Arrangements of Certain Officers.  

(b) On February 18, 2015, Flowserve Corporation (the “Company”) announced that Michael S. Taff, Senior Vice President and Chief Financial Officer of the Company will resign effective March 31, 2015.  

The Company has commenced a comprehensive search for a new Chief Financial Officer. The Company intends to appoint Malcom Platt, vice president, finance for the Industrial Product Division as interim Chief Financial Officer with primary responsibility for operational finance functions prior to Mr. Taff’s departure.  

A copy of the press release announcing Mr. Taff’s resignation is attached as Exhibit 99.1.
