Section 8 — Other Events  

Item 8.01. Other Events.  

On January 15, 2015, American International Group, Inc. (“AIG”) closed the sale of $1,200,000,000 of AIG’s 3.875% Notes due 2035 (the “2035 Notes”) and $800,000,000 of AIG’s 4.375% Notes due 2055 (the “2055 Notes” and, together with the 2035 Notes, the “Notes”).  

The following documents relating to the sale of the Notes are filed as exhibits to this Current Report on Form 8-K and are incorporated into this Item 8.01 by reference:  

   •   Underwriting Agreement, dated January 12, 2015, between AIG and Citigroup Global Markets Inc., J.P. Morgan Securities LLC, U.S. Bancorp Investments, Inc. and Wells Fargo Securities, LLC, as representatives of the several underwriters named therein;  

   •   Twenty-Third Supplemental Indenture, dated as of January 15, 2015, between AIG and The Bank of New York Mellon, as Trustee;  

   •   Twenty-Fourth Supplemental Indenture, dated as of January 15, 2015, between AIG and The Bank of New York Mellon, as Trustee;  

   •   Form of the 2035 Notes;  

   •   Form of the 2055 Notes;  

   •   Opinion of Sullivan & Cromwell LLP, dated January 15, 2015, as to the validity of the Notes; and  

   •   Opinion of Sullivan & Cromwell LLP, dated January 15, 2015, as to United States federal income tax matters.
