Item 5.02. Departure of Directors or Certain Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of Certain Officers; Compensatory Arrangements of Certain Officers.

On February 5, 2020, Under Armour, Inc. (the “Company”) announced the following organizational change.

Colin Browne, currently the Company’s Chief Supply Chain Officer, has been named Chief Operating Officer of the Company, effective February 17, 2020. In connection with this appointment, the Compensation Committee of the Company’s Board of Directors (the “Compensation Committee”) approved an increase of Mr. Browne’s annual base salary from $580,294 to $700,000. 

A summary of Mr. Browne’s business experience is available in the Company’s previously filed Annual Report on Form 10-K for the fiscal year ended December 31, 2018.

A copy of the Company’s press release announcing the appointment of Mr. Browne is attached hereto as Exhibit 99.1 and is incorporated by reference herein.

Item 8.01. Other Events.

On February 5, 2020, the Company also announced that Paul Fipps, currently the Company’s Chief Digital Officer (and a named executive officer), has been named Chief Experience Officer of the Company, effective February 17, 2020. 

A copy of the Company’s press release announcing the appointment of Mr. Fipps is attached hereto as Exhibit 99.1 and is incorporated by reference herein.
