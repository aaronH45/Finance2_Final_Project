Item 7.01Regulation FD

On January 8, 2020, Transocean Ltd. announced that Transocean Inc., its wholly-owned subsidiary, commenced an offering (the “Offering”) of U.S. $750 million aggregate principal amount of senior unsecured notes due 2027 (the “Notes”) to eligible purchasers pursuant to Rule 144A/Regulation S. The Notes will be guaranteed by Transocean Ltd. and certain of Transocean Inc.’s subsidiaries.

The company intends to use the net proceeds from the Offering to refinance, repurchase and/or redeem certain of its existing indebtedness in one or more transactions, and/or for general corporate purposes.

A copy of the press release announcing the Offering is furnished herewith as Exhibit 99.1 and is incorporated herein by reference. 

​

In connection with the Offering, the company disclosed that it estimated, as of December 31, 2019, it had approximately $3.0 billion of total liquidity (including $1.3 billion of available borrowing capacity under its secured revolving credit facility, which is currently undrawn.)
