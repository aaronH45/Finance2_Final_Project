Item 5.03.Amendment to Articles of Incorporation or Bylaws; Change in Fiscal Year.

On January 15, 2020, the Board of Directors of Micron Technology, Inc. (the “Company”) amended and restated the Company's bylaws (the “Amended and Restated Bylaws”) to decrease the size of the Board of Directors from eight to seven. This description of the amendment to the Company's bylaws is qualified in its entirety by reference to the text of the Amended and Restated Bylaws filed as Exhibit 99.1 to this report.

Proposal 1

The following nominees for Directors were elected. Each person elected as a Director will serve until the next annual meeting of shareholders or until such person’s successor is elected and qualified.
