Item 5.02. Departure of Directors or Certain Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of Certain Officers; Compensation Arrangements of Certain Officers.

This is an amendment to Item 5.02 of the Form 8-K that was filed by McDonald’s Corporation (the “Company”) on December 10, 2019, which reported that on December 6, 2019 the Company’s Board of Directors (the “Board”) elected Catherine M.
