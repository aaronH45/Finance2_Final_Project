 Item 7.01.  

Regulation FD Disclosure.

On February 12, 2020, United Rentals, Inc. issued a press release (attached hereto as Exhibit 99.1) announcing that members of its senior management will present at the Citi 2020 Global Industrials Conference on Thursday, February 20, 2020. Such presentations may include a discussion of the Company’s business, financial performance and forward-looking information.
