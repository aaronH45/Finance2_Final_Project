Item 8.01Other Events.

On January 29, 2020, the Board of Directors of Capital One Financial Corporation (the “Company”) declared a quarterly common stock dividend of $0.40 per share. The dividend is payable on February 21, 2020 to stockholders of record at the close of business on February 10, 2020.

The Board of Directors also approved quarterly dividends on the Company’s 6.00% fixed rate non-cumulative perpetual preferred stock, Series B, the Company’s 6.20% fixed rate non-cumulative perpetual preferred stock, Series F, the Company’s 5.20% fixed rate non-cumulative perpetual preferred stock, Series G, the Company’s 6.00% fixed rate non-cumulative perpetual preferred stock, Series H, and the Company’s 5.00% fixed rate non-cumulative perpetual preferred stock, Series I. These dividends are payable on March 2, 2020 to stockholders of record at the close of business on February 14, 2020.

Based on these declarations, the Company will pay approximately $186 million in common stock dividends and approximately $55 million in total preferred stock dividends in the first quarter of 2020. Under the terms of the Company’s outstanding preferred stock, the Company’s ability to pay dividends on, make distributions with respect to, or to repurchase, redeem or acquire its common stock or any preferred stock ranking on parity with or junior to the preferred stock, is subject to restrictions in the event that the Company does not declare and either pay or set aside a sum sufficient for payment of dividends on the preferred stock for the immediately preceding dividend period.
