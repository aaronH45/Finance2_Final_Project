Item 5.03 – Amendments to Articles of Incorporation or Bylaws; Change in Fiscal Year.  

Effective January 20, 2020, the Board of Directors of Stanley Black & Decker, Inc. (the “Company”) approved an amendment to the Amended and Restated Bylaws of the Company to lower the threshold ownership requirement to call a special meeting of the shareholders from 35% to 25% of the voting power of all shares entitled to vote on any issue proposed to be considered at the meeting.  

The above summary is qualified in its entirety by reference to the full text of the Amended and Restated Bylaws, a copy of which is filed as Exhibit 3.1 to this Current Report on Form 8-K and is incorporated by reference herein.
