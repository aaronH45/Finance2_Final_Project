 Item 5.02 

Departure of Directors or Certain Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of Certain Officers; Compensatory Arrangements of Certain Officers. 

On December 19, 2019, Nielsen Holdings plc (the “Company”) filed a Current Report on Form 8-K (the “Original 8-K”)  disclosing that the Board of Directors (the “Board”) of the Company appointed Ms. Nancy Tellem as a member of the Board, effective December 18, 2019. Committee assignments for Ms. Tellem were not determined as of the filing of the Original 8-K.  

On January 22, 2020, the Board appointed Ms. Tellem to the Compensation Committee of the Board, effective February 3, 2020, filling the Compensation Committee vacancy created by Robert C. Pozen’s resignation as a member thereof. Mr. Pozen was appointed by the Board to serve on the Audit Committee of the Board, effective February 3, 2020, filling the Audit Committee vacancy created by David Rawlinson’s resignation as a member thereof. Mr. Rawlinson will continue to serve on the Board and will serve as Chief Executive Officer, Nielsen Connect, effective February 3, 2020.
