Item 8.01 Other Events

On February 11, 2020, Pfizer Inc. (“Pfizer”) issued a notice for the redemption in full of all $1,065,000,000 aggregate principal amount outstanding of its 4.20% Notes due 2047 (ISIN: XS1572326145; COMMON CODE: 157232614) (the “Notes”). 

The Notes will be redeemed on March 17, 2020 at a “make-whole” redemption price as set forth in the indenture, as supplemented, pursuant to which the Notes were issued. On the redemption date, the redemption price will become due and payable on the Notes and, unless Pfizer defaults in the payment of the redemption price and accrued and unpaid interest, interest on the Notes will cease to accrue on and after the redemption date. The notice of redemption specifying the terms, conditions and procedures for the redemption is available through The Bank of New York Mellon, located at 240 Greenwich St., New York, New York 10286, as paying agent for the Notes. The foregoing does not constitute a notice of redemption for the Notes.
