Item 5.02 Departure of Directors or Certain Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of Certain Officers. 

On February 4, 2020, Chairman and Chief Executive Officer Luke Kissam advised the Board of Directors of Albemarle Corporation (the “Company”) that he will retire from his roles as an officer and director of the Company effective June, 2020, for health reasons. On February 5, 2020, the Company issued a press release with respect thereto and announced that the Board of Directors will be conducting a comprehensive search process with respect to a successor to Mr. Kissam as Chief Executive Officer, which will include internal and external candidates. A copy of the press release is filed as Exhibit 99.1 hereto and incorporated herein by reference.
