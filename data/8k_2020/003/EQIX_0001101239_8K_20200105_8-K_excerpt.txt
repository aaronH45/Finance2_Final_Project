 Item 5.02.  

Election of Director.   

Equinix, Inc. (“Equinix”) has increased the size of its Board of Directors (“Board”) to 11 and Adaire Fox-Martin has been elected as an independent director to Board, effective January 5, 2020. With the election of Ms. Fox-Martin, Equinix’s Board now consists of 11 members. Ms. Fox-Martin will serve as a member of the Board’s Nominating Committee.  

As a member of Equinix’s Board, Ms. Fox-Martin will receive Equinix’s standard compensation for non-employee  directors.  

 Item 8.01.  

Other Events.   

On January 7, 2020 Equinix issued a press release announcing the election of Ms. Fox-Martin to the Board. A copy of the press release is furnished as Exhibit 99.1 to this Form 8-K and is incorporated herein by reference.
