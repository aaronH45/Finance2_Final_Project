Assets Under Management

Atlanta, January 13, 2020 --- Invesco Ltd. (NYSE: IVZ) today reported preliminary month-end assets under management (AUM) of $1,226.2 billion, an increase of 2.0% versus previous month-end. The increase was driven by reinvested distributions, favorable market returns, foreign exchange, and net inflows in money market and non-management fee earning AUM, partially offset by net long-term outflows. Reinvested distributions and FX increased AUM by $12.9 billion and $4.0 billion, respectively. Preliminary average total AUM for the quarter through December 31 were $1,200.6 billion, and preliminary average active AUM for the quarter through December 31 were $914.2 billion.
