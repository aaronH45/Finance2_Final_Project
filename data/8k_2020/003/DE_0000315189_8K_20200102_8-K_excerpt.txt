DEERE CES ANALYST DAY TO BE BROADCAST ON WEBSITE 

MOLINE, IL -- (2 January 2020) Deere & Company today said its 2020 

CES analyst day would be broadcast over the internet on Wednesday, 

8 January, beginning at 10:30 a.m. central time. During the call, the company’s 

Chief Executive Officer and Chief Financial Officer will discuss the company’s 

strategy and operating performance. 

The webcast can be accessed at www.JohnDeere.com/earnings. The 

recorded webcast will be available on the Deere website for a period of time 

afterward. The slides referred to in the webcast can be accessed at the link 

above.
