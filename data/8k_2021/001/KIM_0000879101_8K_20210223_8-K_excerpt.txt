  Item 8.01.   Other Events    

On February 23, 2021, Kimco Realty Corporation (the “Company”) filed an automatic shelf registration statement on Form S-3 (File No. 333-253404) (the “Registration Statement”) with the Securities and Exchange Commission (the “SEC”).
