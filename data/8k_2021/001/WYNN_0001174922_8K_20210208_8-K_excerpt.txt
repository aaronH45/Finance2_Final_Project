 Item 7.01.  

Regulation FD Disclosure.  

On February 8, 2021, Wynn Resorts, Limited (“Wynn Resorts” or the “Company”) commenced an underwritten public offering of 5,500,000 shares of its common stock (the “Offering”). Wynn Resorts intends to grant the underwriters a 30-day option to purchase up to 825,000 shares of common stock. Wynn Resorts expects to use the net proceeds from the Offering for general corporate purposes. The Offering is subject to market and other conditions. The press release relating to the Offering is attached hereto as Exhibit 99.1 and is incorporated herein by reference.  

 Item 8.01.  

Other Events.  

In connection with the Offering, Wynn Resorts provided supplemental risk factors to potential investors. The supplemental risk factors are attached hereto as Exhibit 99.2 and are incorporated herein by reference.
