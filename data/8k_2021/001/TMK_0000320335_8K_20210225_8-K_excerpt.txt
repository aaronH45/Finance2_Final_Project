Item 5.03 Amendments to Articles of Incorporation or Bylaws; Change in Fiscal Year.

On February 24, 2021, the Board of Directors (the “Board”) of Globe Life Inc. (the “Company”) amended and restated the Company’s Amended and Restated By-laws (the “By-Laws”) to implement proxy access, effective immediately. 

The By-Laws include a new Article III, Section 3 that permits a shareholder, or a group of up to 20 shareholders, owning at least three percent of the Company’s outstanding stock continuously for at least three years to nominate and include in the Company’s annual meeting proxy materials director nominees constituting up to the greater of two directors or twenty percent of the Board, provided that the shareholders and nominees satisfy the requirements specified in the By-Laws.

The By-Laws also contain other non-material conforming, clarifying, and updating changes.

The foregoing description is qualified in its entirety by reference to the full text of the By-Laws, which is attached hereto as Exhibit 3.2.
