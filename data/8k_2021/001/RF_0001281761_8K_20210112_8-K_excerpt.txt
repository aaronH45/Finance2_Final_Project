 Item 7.01.  

Regulation FD Disclosure  

On January 12, 2021, Regions Financial Corporation (“Regions”) is sending redemption notices to the holders of its 3.200% Senior Notes due February 8, 2021 (the “Notes”), which will result in the redemption on January 22, 2021 (the “Redemption Date”) of the Notes pursuant to the terms of the Indenture, dated as of August 8, 2005, between Regions, as issuer, and Deutsche Bank Trust Company Americas, as trustee (the “Trustee”), as supplemented by the Seventh Supplemental Indenture, dated as of February 8, 2016, between Regions and the Trustee, at an aggregate redemption price equal to 100% of the principal amount of the Notes to be redeemed. Holders will also receive accrued interest thereon to the Redemption Date.  

In accordance with general instruction B.2 of Form 8-K, this information is being furnished and shall not be deemed filed for purposes of Section 18 of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934.
