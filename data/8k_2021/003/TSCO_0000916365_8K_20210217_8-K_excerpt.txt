Item 8.01 Other Events.

On February 17, 2021, Tractor Supply Company (the "Company") issued a press release announcing an agreement to acquire all of the outstanding equity interests of Orscheln Farm and Home, LLC, a farm and ranch retailer with 167 stores in 11 states.
