Item 5.02Departure of Directors or Certain Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of Certain Officers; Compensatory Arrangements of Certain Officers.

On February 19, 2021, the Human Resources Committee (the “Committee”) of the Board of Directors of Constellation Brands, Inc. (“Constellation” or the “Company”), approved changes to the compensation arrangements for Garth Hankinson, the Company’s Chief Financial Officer. In recognition of his individual performance achievements, overall responsibilities, and functional experience, the Committee approved an increase to his annual base salary from $603,750 to $715,000. This adjustment is effective as of March 1, 2021.
