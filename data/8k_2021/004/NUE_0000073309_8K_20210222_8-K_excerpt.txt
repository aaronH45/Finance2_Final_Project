 Item 5.03.  

Amendments to Articles of Incorporation or Bylaws; Change in Fiscal Year.  

(a) On February 22, 2021, the Board of Directors (the “Board”) of Nucor Corporation (the “Company”), approved an amendment and restatement (the “Amendment”) of the Company’s Amended and Restated Bylaws (as amended and restated, the “Bylaws”) that became effective immediately upon approval by the Board. The Amendment adds proxy access provisions to the Company’s Bylaws, which permit a stockholder, or group of up to 20 stockholders, owning at least 3% of the Company’s outstanding stock continuously for at least three years, to nominate and include in the Company’s proxy materials for an annual meeting of stockholders, a number of director nominees equal to the greater of (i) two nominees or (ii) 20% of the Board, provided that the stockholder(s) and the director nominee(s) satisfy the requirements specified in the Bylaws.  

In addition to the provisions described above, the Amendment includes various conforming and technical changes to the Bylaws. The foregoing description of the terms and conditions of the Amendment and the Bylaws does not purport to be complete and is qualified in its entirety by reference to the full text of the Bylaws, a copy of which is filed as Exhibit 3.1 hereto and is incorporated herein by reference.
