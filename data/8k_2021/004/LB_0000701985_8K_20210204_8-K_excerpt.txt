Item 5.02. Departure of Directors or Certain Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of Certain Officers; Compensatory Arrangements of Certain Officers.

Stuart Burgdoerfer is retiring and will no longer serve as Executive Vice President and Chief Financial Officer as of the end of August 2021.

On February 4, 2021, L Brands, Inc. issued a press release announcing Mr. Burgdoerfer’s retirement, which also included guidance with respect to fourth quarter 2020 comparable sales and updated guidance with respect to fourth quarter 2020 earnings. A copy of the press release is attached hereto as Exhibit 99.1 and is incorporated by reference herein except with regards to the third paragraph therein regarding fourth quarter 2020 guidance.
