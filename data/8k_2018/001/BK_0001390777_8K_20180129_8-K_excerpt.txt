ITEM 8.01. OTHER EVENTS.  

On January 29, 2018, The Bank of New York Mellon Corporation (the “Company”) issued $1,000,000,000 aggregate principal amount of its 2.950% Senior Medium-Term Notes Series J due 2023 (the “5-year Senior Notes”) and $750,000,000 aggregate principal amount of its 3.400% Senior Medium-Term Notes Series J due 2028 (the “10-year Senior Notes” and, together with the 5-year Senior Notes, the “Notes”). The Notes were registered under the Securities Act of 1933, as amended, pursuant to a registration statement on Form S-3 (File No. 333-209450). In connection with this issuance, the form of Fixed Rate Senior Medium-Term Notes Series J and the legal opinion as to the legality of the Notes are being filed as Exhibit 4.1 and Exhibit 5.1, respectively, to this report.
