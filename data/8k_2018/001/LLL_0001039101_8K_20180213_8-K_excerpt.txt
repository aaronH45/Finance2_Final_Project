 Item 5.03  Amendments to Articles of Incorporation or Bylaws; Change in Fiscal Year.     

On February 13, 2018, the Board of Directors (the “Board”) of L3 Technologies, Inc. (the “Company”) adopted an amendment and restatement of the Company’s Amended and Restated Bylaws (the “Bylaws”) to eliminate the requirement in Section 3.3 of Article III thereof that a meeting of the Board be held immediately prior to or after each annual meeting of stockholders. 

The foregoing description of the amendment to the Bylaws is qualified in its entirety by reference to the full text of the Bylaws, which are filed as Exhibit 3.2 hereto and incorporated herein by reference.
