 Item 8.01. Other Events.  

On January 29, 2018, Hologic, Inc. (“Hologic” or the “Company”) issued a press release announcing (i) that the Company has commenced an offer to repurchase the Company’s 2.00% Convertible Senior Notes due 2042 (the “Notes”) at the option of holders and (ii) a redemption of the Notes. The Company also announced that it has irrevocably elected to settle any conversion of the Notes entirely in cash. A copy of the press release is attached hereto as Exhibit 99.1 and is incorporated herein in its entirety by reference.  

This Current Report on Form 8-K is not an offer to purchase, nor the solicitation of an offer to sell, securities, nor a solicitation for acceptance of the offer to repurchase the Notes.
