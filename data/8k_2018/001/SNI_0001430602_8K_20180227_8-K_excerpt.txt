 Item 8.01 Other Events  

On February 27, Scripps Networks Interactive, Inc. (“Scripps”) and Discovery Communications, Inc. (“Discovery”) issued a press release informing Scripps shareholders that (1) Scripps’ previously announced merger with Discovery (the “Merger”), pursuant to the Agreement and Plan of Merger, dated as of July 30, 2017, by and between Scripps, Discover and Skylight Merger Sub, Inc. (the “Merger Agreement”), is expected to close on March 6, 2018 and (2) the election deadline for holders of Common Voting Shares and Class A Common Shares to elect the form of consideration they wish to receive in the Merger, subject to the proration and cash top-up procedures set forth in the Merger Agreement, will be 5:00 p.m., New York City Time, on March 2, 2018, unless extended.  

A copy of the press release is filed as Exhibit 99.1 hereto and is incorporated herein by reference.
