Item 5.02 Departure of Directors or Certain Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of Certain Officers; Compensatory Arrangements of Certain Officers.

As previously announced, Viktoras R. Sekmakas resigned as Executive Vice President of PPG Industries, Inc. (the “Company”), effective September 30, 2017, and elected to retire from the Company. 

In connection with his departure, the Company and Mr. Sekmakas have entered into a Separation Agreement. Under the Separation Agreement, Mr. Sekmakas’s employment with the Company will end on February 28, 2018, and he will retire from the Company on March 1, 2018. Pursuant to the Separation Agreement, Mr. Sekmakas will receive a lump-sum separation payment of $641,128 and a lump-sum payment of $448,875 in lieu of his annual bonus for 2017. Mr. Sekmakas’s previously awarded stock options, restricted stock units and total shareholder return contingent shares will vest in accordance with their terms.
