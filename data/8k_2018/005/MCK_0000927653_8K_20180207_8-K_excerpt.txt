 Item 8.01 Other Events.  

On June 16, 2017, McKesson Corporation (the “Company”) filed its Definitive Proxy Statement (the “Proxy Statement”) related to its annual meeting of stockholders to be held on July 26, 2017. Subsequent to the filing of the Proxy Statement, the Company discovered a typographical error in the section entitled “Shareholder Proposals for the 2018 Annual Meeting.” The Proxy Statement states that the deadline for stockholders to submit a proposal to be included in the proxy statement for the 2018 annual meeting is February 8, 2018. The correct deadline is February 16, 2018. This filing does not change any other information in the Proxy Statement as originally filed.
