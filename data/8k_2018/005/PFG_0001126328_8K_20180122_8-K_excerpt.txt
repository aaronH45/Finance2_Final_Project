Item 7.01 Regulation FD Disclosure

This report is filed quarterly to disclose assets under management (“AUM”) by asset manager, prior to the availability of Principal Financial Group, Inc's (the "Company") quarterly earnings release. The amounts presented herein will be consistent with the format of AUM by asset manager and presented again in such format within the Company's financial supplement for the quarter ended December 31, 2017 when that document is posted to the Company's investor relations web site on or about January 29, 2018. 

As of December 31, 2017, the AUM by asset manager were $430.9 billion for Principal Global Investors and $163.6 billion for Other Entities of the Company. Other Entities of the Company include Principal International, Principal Bank and Principal Life Insurance Company.
