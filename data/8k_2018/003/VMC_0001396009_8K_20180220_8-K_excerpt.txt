 Item 8.01. Other Events.  

On February 20, 2018, Vulcan Materials Company (the “Company”) announced the pricing of its offering of $350 million aggregate principal amount of 4.70% Notes due 2048 (the “2048 Notes”). A copy of the Company’s press release issued in connection with this announcement is attached hereto as Exhibit 99.2 and incorporated herein by reference.
