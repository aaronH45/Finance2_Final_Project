ITEM 2.02 RESULTS OF OPERATIONS AND FINANCIAL CONDITION. 

On February 9, 2018, Intuit Inc. (“Intuit”) issued a press release updating its financial outlook for the quarter ended January 31, 2018 due to the late start to the tax season, raising its full year earnings per share guidance due to new U.S. tax legislation, and reiterating its full-year revenue and operating income guidance for the 2018 fiscal year.
