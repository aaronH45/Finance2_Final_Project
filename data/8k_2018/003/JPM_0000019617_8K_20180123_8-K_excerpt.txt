 Item 8.01. Other Events  

On January 23, 2018, JPMorgan Chase & Co. closed a public offering of $2,250,000,000 aggregate principal amount of its Fixed-to-Floating Rate Notes due 2029 and $1,750,000,000 aggregate principal amount of its Fixed-to-Floating Rate Notes due 2049 (collectively, the “Notes”).
