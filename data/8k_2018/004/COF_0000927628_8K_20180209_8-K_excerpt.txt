Item 8.01 Other Events. 

On February 1, 2018, the Board of Directors of Capital One Financial Corporation (the “Company”) declared a quarterly dividend of $0.40 per share on the outstanding shares of the Company’s common stock. The dividend is payable on February 23, 2018 to stockholders of record at the close of business on February 12, 2018.

The Company has outstanding warrants to purchase common stock of the Company, expiring November 14, 2018. In accordance with the terms of those outstanding warrants, the declaration of a dividend in excess of $0.375 per share will result in an adjustment to the warrant exercise price, which will be reduced from $41.979 to $41.968 per share effective as of the close of business on February 12, 2018.

Further information regarding the warrants and adjustments to the warrant exercise price is available on the Company’s website (http://www.capitalone.com). Choose “About Us,” then choose “Investors,” then choose “Warrant Information” under the heading “Stock Information.”
