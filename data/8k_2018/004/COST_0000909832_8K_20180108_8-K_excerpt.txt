 Item 5.02 Departure of Directors or Certain Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of Certain Officers; Compensatory Arrangements of Certain Officers  

Dennis Zook, Executive Vice President, Southwest Division and Mexico, and John McKay, Executive Vice President, Northern Division, are retiring from the Company. The Company expresses its deep gratitude to Messrs. Zook and McKay for their thirty-plus years of service and numerous contributions to the Company’s success.  

Russ Miller, currently Senior Vice President and General Manager — Western Canada Region, has been appointed Executive Vice President, Southwest Division and Mexico. Jim Klauer, currently Senior Vice President, Merchandising — Non-Foods and E-Commerce, has been appointed Executive Vice President, Northern Division. Both will report to the President and Chief Executive Officer, Craig Jelinek.  

These changes are effective March 2, 2018.
