 Item 8.01 Other Events  

 On January 4, 2018, UDR, Inc. (the “Company”) announced that Thomas W. Toomey, the Company’s Chief Executive Officer and President, has been appointed Chairman of the Board of Directors of the Company. The Company also established a Lead Independent Director position and appointed James D. Klingbeil, who had previously served as Chairman of the Board of Directors, as Lead Independent Director. The Company’s press release with respect to such actions is filed as Exhibit 99.1 to this Form 8-K.
